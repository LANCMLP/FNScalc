Food Stamps Eligibility Calculator

Use this calculator to find out if you may be eligible for SNAP and an estimated amount of benefits you could receive.

Filling out this tool will take only about five minutes.

Does your household include someone between the ages of 18-49? *required

Members of your household who are age 18-49 may need to either be working or searching for work in order to be included in the household for more than three months. If they are unable to work, there are a number of exceptions to this rule, including disability and having a child. More information about work requirements will be available with the results of this calculator.
